Attributes used:

  • controls: Adds playback controls (play, pause, volume).
  • autoplay: Automatically plays the audio when the page loads.
  • loop: Replays the audio after it finishes.
  • muted: Starts the audio muted.
  • preload: Specifies how the browser should preload the audio (e.g., metadata).
